About Beloved

Beloved was founded by Whanderlust and Kankati on September 19th, 2014. We were a YMCK guild (now MYBCKN) from the game Maple Story that started as a guild for socializing and bossing, to something like that of a small community today.

Our guild does the usual bossing and socializing, but we also play other games together too such as League of Legends and Minecraft.
